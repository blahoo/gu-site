What’s this?

Apocalypse was, at the time, the largest high school hackathon in Canada (and the first Hack Club Flagship event in Canada too) where we hosted over 180 attendees at Shopify HQ in Toronto over 72 hours. In my eyes, this will be the most peak hackathon I’ve been to; it’s important to note that the core ethos of Apocalypse which makes it so special wasn’t really anything to do with the prizes, but the atmosphere, community, the people. The goal was to avoid the often competitive nature of hackathon and go all in on community building with cool zombified theming. 

Of course, this event wouldn’t have been possible with one of most high agency and committed teams I’m proud to be a part of: Mutammin, Arav, Acon, Sam, Shimmy, Ev, Vivian, Ryan, Sarvesh
