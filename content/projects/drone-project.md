# Autonomous Emergency Response Drone

![Drone Technology](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1473968512647-3e447244af8f?w=600&h=400&fit=crop)

Developing autonomous drones for emergency response scenarios taught me that the most complex technology often serves the simplest human needs - saving lives.

## The Mission

Create a drone system that could:
- Navigate disaster zones autonomously
- Provide real-time mapping for rescue operations
- Operate in GPS-denied environments
- Stream critical data to emergency responders

## Technical Challenges

The hardest part wasn't the flight control - it was teaching the drone to make decisions in chaotic, unpredictable environments.

## Impact

While still in development, early tests showed promising results for search and rescue applications. Sometimes the best engineering happens when human stakes are highest.
